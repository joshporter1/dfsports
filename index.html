DFS Terminology

DFS blogs, podcasts, and websites use a few terms that may mean something in other contexts. Below are the "DFS" definitions.

Game Terms
  • DFS Daily Fantasy Sports
  • Cash games contests that pay out smaller amounts to a broad swath of the entrants
  • GPP Guaranteed Prize Pool, a game type where the prizes are guaranteed no matter what (even if the lobby doesn't fill). These typically pay out the top ~10% in larger amounts
  • 50/50 a game type where the top 50% of entrants are paid out the same amount (usually double the entry fee minus commission)
  • Double-up a game type where roughly the top 40% of players are paid out double the entry fee
  • Head-to-head a game type where you play a person 1v1. Winner gets double minus commission
Strategy and Player terms
  • Chalk players that are likely to be high-owned (ex: Antonio Brown vs the Browns)
  • Fade to avoid a player because you think they will be highly owned or underperform
  • Value the amount of points a player is projected to score in relation to their salary
